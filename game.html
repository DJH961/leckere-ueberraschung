Nazan Dodge

You are a Nazan. The other Nazans are bouncing — and they want a hug you do not want.

Move with your mouse, finger, or the arrow keys / WASD. Survive as long as you can — more Nazans keep joining.

Cornered? Grab a power-up when one appears. Timed ones kick in the instant you grab them — eating mode, invisibility, tiny mode, snail mode, a Force Field that wraps you in a bubble and firmly bumps Nazans off its skin (one brushed in open space stays in play; steer the bubble so it shoves a Nazan against a nearby wall and it sails out of bounds), though you slow to a crawl while it's up, so it's a push you have to commit to — or a golden Star that makes you invincible and faster, leaving a sparkling dash-trail as you barge straight through Nazans for points — drive one into the edge and it sails clean off the screen. Shove and Boom are special: you bank them and unleash them when the moment's right by tapping your own Nazan (or pressing Space), no aiming needed — but you can't sit on them forever: each pulses a gentle warning and auto-fires on its own if you hold it too long. Shove blasts nearby Nazans away and Boom pops the whole crowd around you for points. The Chain Zap now fires the instant you grab it, forking lightning to the nearest Nazans and vaporising them — it auto-targets, clearing just over half a crowd (and a light board outright) in one strike. Shove, Star and the Force Field push Nazans without destroying them, so guide them off the edge yourself. Only one power-up appears at a time, and the next won't show up until you've spent the last.

Move with your finger or keyboard. Grab power-ups to survive; tap your Nazan to fire Shove or Boom (they auto-fire if held too long). Chain Zap fires as soon as you pick it up.

How to play

Dodge the bouncing Nazans for as long as you can. The longer you last, the higher your score.

Controls

Move with your mouse, finger, or the arrow keys / WASD.

Fire a banked power-up (Shove / Boom) by tapping your own Nazan or pressing Space — no aiming needed. Banked power-ups auto-fire if you hold them too long. Press Esc to leave the game.

Power-ups

Only one appears at a time, and the next won't show up until you've spent the last. Shove, Star and the Force Field push Nazans off-screen without destroying them — and every Nazan you shove away still banks points, so a push is never wasted. They always wander back in (a little slower than they left), so a push buys space and a few points rather than a permanent kill.

  • 😋Eating ModeDevour Nazans on contact — they flee, so chase them down.
  • 👻InvisibleNazans pass straight through you for a few seconds.
  • 🤏Tiny ModeShrink down and slip through the crowd.
  • 🐌Snail ModeEvery other Nazan crawls in slow motion.
  • 🛡️Force FieldA bubble that gently pushes any Nazan that touches it clean off the screen — and scores a few points each time. You slow to a crawl while it's up, so it's a defensive shield, not a free clear.
  • ⭐Star PowerInvincible and faster, with a sparkling trail — barge straight through Nazans to shove them aside for points.
  • 💨ShoveBanked. Blasts nearby Nazans away and banks points for every one it catches. Tap your Nazan or press Space.
  • 💥BoomBanked. Pops the whole crowd around you for points. Tap your Nazan or press Space.
  • ⚡Chain ZapFires the instant you grab it, forking lightning to the nearest Nazans and vaporising them.

Egan Mode

Once you've banked around 1,000 points of normal play, the screen clears for a bonus round: Egan Mode. After that it returns every so often, but the gap between rounds widens each time (1,000, then +1,500, +2,000…), so the deeper you push the rarer — and more precious — they get.

  • 🥚Catch the EgansEgans fly across the arena in curving arcs — sweep your Nazan over them to catch them. They start big and slow and turn smaller and faster as the round goes on.
  • 🔗Chain for combosCatch Egans in quick succession to build a points multiplier (up to 3×). Let too long pass between catches and the combo resets.
  • 🌟Golden EgansTwo special Golden Egans appear each round, worth a fat bonus — grab them while your combo is hot.
  • 🎁Catch targetNet 25 Egans in a single round and a free power-up is waiting for you the moment you're back in the game.

Difficulty & intensity

Sub-1,000 play is gentle and unchanged. But every time you complete an Egan round the game steps up a notch — Nazans arrive faster and more often, the board holds a few more of them, and the music quickens — with a subtle further creep as you climb through each stage. The further you get, the more relentless it becomes, so lean on your power-ups and Egan rounds to keep up.

On mobile

On phones and tablets your Nazan vibrates at the moments that matter — firing a power-up, grabbing a pickup, getting a warning buzz as a timed power-up runs low, and dying — so you get a little physical feedback to play by. Use the 📳 button to switch the vibration on or off.

Make it yours

Tap the ‹ › arrows on the start and game-over cards to pick which Nazan you play as — your choice is remembered for next time. Each Nazan even has their own voice, so they pipe up as they bounce. Prefer to play in peace? The 🔊 button mutes every sound, including the procedural background music.

Tips & tricks

  • 🎯Hug the middle. Nazans spawn from the edges, so the centre gives you the most reaction time — and keeps you within reach of whichever power-up pops up.
  • 🌀Shoving scores — and buys room. Knocking Nazans out of bounds with Shove, Star or the Force Field banks points for every one you displace and clears space fast. They always wander back, though, so treat it as points-plus-a-reset rather than a finisher.
  • 🚗Don't bother aiming. A banked Shove or Boom auto-fires the instant a Nazan would hit you, so just barrel into a crowd — it doubles as a free panic button that saves you from an otherwise lethal touch.
  • 🍽️Grab everything, even the gentle buffs. Every non-killer you collect quietly raises the odds that a board-clearing Eating Mode, Boom or Chain Zap shows up next, so keep eating power-ups instead of dodging them.
  • 🔁No back-to-back repeats. A power-up sits out the next two pickups after you grab it, so don't count on Eating Mode twice in a row — spend what you have while you have it.
  • 👴The old Nazans are the deadly ones. Each one speeds up the longer it's on screen, so save your killers for the long-lived veterans rather than fresh arrivals.
  • 💥Let the crowd build, then cash in. The board fills up to a dozen Nazans, so an Eating Mode, Boom or Chain Zap unleashed on a packed screen clears far more — and scores far more — than one fired early.

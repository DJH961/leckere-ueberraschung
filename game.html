Nazan Dodge

You are a Nazan. The other Nazans are bouncing — and they want a hug you do not want.

Move with your mouse, finger, or the arrow keys / WASD. Survive as long as you can — more Nazans keep joining.

Cornered? Grab a power-up when one pops up — then tap your own Nazan (or press Space) to unleash it. Flavours include eating mode, shove, invisibility, freeze and snail mode. Only one appears at a time, and the next won't show up until you've spent the last.
